How We Came to Be

One day, Tom dropped his phone. He took it into his local tech repair company and they were charging him nearly $500 to fix it! Tom thought about how rigged that was to rip off customers because they couldn't do it themselves. He created Tom's Tech Repair to fix customers devices cheaper than all the competition.

Our Mission

Tom's Tech Repair seeks to bring cheap technology repair to you. We strive to grow our business with friendliness and compassion. We will not overcharge, and can work with what is best for you. So come to your nearest location for all your tech repair needs!
